Summer offer project

Posted on June 23, 2024

Very Excited to share my first project on pattern design for Siesta o'Clock, a Goa (India) based Clothing brand. Siesta means a short sleep or rest that people take in the afternoon, especially in hot countries.

The owners of the brand went through my instagram page & gave me opportunity to design 2 patterns for their label. The YELLOW one is inspired from the beach life "They float in their own way" & the PINK one is motivated from the "Hiran Mithila Paintings" from India.
